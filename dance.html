Dance is a huge part of who I am, and is one of my biggest passions! These are some dance sets/video projects I've had the privilege of being apart of. You can also watch me dance on Instagram via my story here under "wiggles", or on my dance account here!

Dance Journey

I love open-style dance & choreography! Although I got my start pretty late at the beginning of my college career, I've fallen completely in love with movement and it has quickly become one of the biggest passions in my life! Feel free to ask me about my favorite open-style choreographers and dance teams, or any of my past performances and teams!

Abba Modern: Spring 2019 - Present

Abba Modern, the Christian dance team on the UC Berkeley campus, was my first dance team ever! This team was my introduction into dance and is a huge reason why I fell so deeply in love with dance. Abba has pushed me in my abilities to pick up choreography, tell stories through dance, choreograph pieces, and lead. I served as the Executive Director of the team for the 2020-2021 school year.

TrueLement: Spring 2020 - Present

TrueLement, or TL, is an open-style exhibition team, that dances at a higher level than training teams. TL has been a team that I have aspired to join since first starting to dance, and so it has been a great honor and pleasure to dance along people I have looked up to. This team has challenged and pushed me to grow immensely in my dance skills, pick up, and vocabulary.

The [M]ovement: Summer 2019 - Spring 2020

The [M]ovement has been a dance home for me to be vulnerable, grow a sense of community, and foster my leadership skills. I was able to participate in both live sets and video projects, as well as be apart of CORE, the logistics board that leads the organization. I served as the Social Chair (Fall 2019) and External Affairs Chair (Spring 2020). I currently serve as an open-style choreographer and team director.

AFX: Summer 2019 & Fall 2020

I also participated in a few teams under AFX, a large umbrella dance organization, where I was able to both build my sense of community and build connections with the greater Berkeley dance community, and train through classes and workshops.
