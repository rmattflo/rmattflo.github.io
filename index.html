About Me

I'm Robb Matthew Flores, but I go by Matt. I am currently a senior at UC Berkeley, where I study Cognitive Science and Design Innovation.

I currently do design work for SimplePractice, Off Their Plate, and Shop Spero and am also pursuing a Berkeley Certificate in Design Innovation. Career-wise, I hope to enter the UI/UX or product design field in the future and have a passion for all things user-centered.

Other than that, I love open-style dance & choreography, sketching & drawing, caligraphy & lettering, food culture and playing video games. Feel free to ask me for recomendations for food/boba, my favorite open-style choreographers and dance teams, my opinions on the direction Pokemon is going towards, or any of my past projects.
