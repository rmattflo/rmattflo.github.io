Mini Bio

I'm Robb Matthew Flores, but I go by Matt. I recently graduated from UC Berkeley, where I studied Cognitive Science and Design Innovation.
Career-wise, I hope to continue working in the product design field to increase accessibility to tech and have a passion for all things user-centered.
