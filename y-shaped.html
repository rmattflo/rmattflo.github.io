Industrial Design & Human Factors Final Project: SHAPED

Research, Graphic Design, Prototyping (Spring 2021)

Collaborators: Ajia Grant, Majd Muna, Jiaji Wu, Alex Yuan

Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Google Drive

In my team's final human-centered industrial engineering design project focused on mitigating musculo-skeletal diseases (MSDs), we wanted to redesign the hold assist for mobile phones. The ultimate goal was to reduce the risk of MSDs and phyiscal fatigue on the fingers and hand experienced when holding a smart phone for prolonged periods of time in such a way that maintains ergonomic standards in respect to human factors while minimizing the physical exposure of using our product.
